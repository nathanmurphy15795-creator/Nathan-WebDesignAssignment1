Album Overview

Below is a summary of Fontaines D.C.'s major albums and a brief review of each.

Album Release Year Review
Dogrel 2019 A raw and electrifying debut that bottles the pulse of Dublin’s post-punk underground. Sharp rhythms and unapologetically local lyricism combine street-level storytelling with poetic swagger, resulting in one of the most confident debuts of the decade.
A Hero's Death 2020 A deliberate step inward, this hypnotic follow-up trades ferocity for introspection. Brooding textures and existential themes create a challenging, mantra-like record that shows the band refusing easy success in favor of artistic growth.
Skinty Fia 2022 Dark, atmospheric, and thematically rich, Skinty Fia explores identity, displacement, and cultural memory. Its immersive production and emotional weight mark it as the band’s most ambitious and mature release.
Romance 2024 Tender and sonically expansive, Romance leans into vulnerability and emotional openness. Dreamy, cinematic soundscapes support poetic reflections on love and loss, showing a band fully comfortable with evolution.
